Return Policy

Eligibility for Returns

We carefully check and verify every product before shipping to ensure quality. We only accept returns under specific conditions:

  • The product received is wrong (not what you ordered).
  • The product received is defective or damaged upon arrival (requires unboxing video proof).

Returns based on change of mind, dislike of the product, or minor cosmetic variations not affecting functionality are generally not accepted.

Return Window

You must initiate the return process within 8 days of the delivery date of your order.

Return Processing Fee

A return processing fee of ₹99 is applicable for all accepted returns. This fee covers handling and logistics.

The fee is either paid in advance during the return initiation process or will be deducted from your refund amount.

How to Initiate a Return

If your order meets the eligibility criteria, please visit our dedicated return initiation page:

Initiate Return Process

You will be guided through the necessary steps, including fee payment and submitting a request form.

Refund Process

Once your return request is approved and the returned product is received and inspected:

  • The refund (minus the ₹99 fee if not paid upfront) will be processed.
  • Refunds are typically issued within 7-10 business days to your original payment method or provided bank account.
  • You will be notified via email or message regarding the status of your refund.

Contact Us

If you have questions about our Return Policy, you can contact our support team via our Contact Us page.
